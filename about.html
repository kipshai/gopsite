About

Pazly is an easy web builder for busy freelancers. Simply drag and drop UI components and build beautiful mobile and web screens.

A perfect tool for your client's first impression. These days working remote means sharing screens, showing and telling. When you first meet your client, use pazly to decide on the web developement requirements. Once you both decide on a layout, download the template and start customizing.
